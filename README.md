## What Is This?

*The EB-2 NIW Framework* is a structured technical resource for interpreting and applying the *Matter of Dhanasar* standard (AAO 2016) within U.S. EB-2 National Interest Waiver adjudication.

This framework organizes the NIW pathway into a decision-oriented structure for qualified professionals assessing eligibility, evaluating evidentiary position, and identifying RFE risk patterns before filing.

**This is an educational resource. It is not legal advice.** Before filing any immigration petition, retain a licensed immigration attorney. ImigraScore is not a law firm.

## What This Framework Covers

- **EB-2 Eligibility Thresholds** — Advanced Degree Professional and Exceptional Ability pathways under INA Section 203(b)(2)
- **The Three-Prong Dhanasar Test** — Substantial Merit and National Importance; Well Positioned; Beneficial to Waive
- **Proposed Endeavor Construction** — How to build a specific, defensible plan of work and the four structural failures that trigger RFEs
- **Evidentiary Strategy** — What counts as third-party validation, establishing national importance, documenting execution capacity
- **RFE Risk Signals** — Organized by Dhanasar prong with preemption strategies
- **Self-Assessment Framework** — Decision trees for evaluating petition strength before filing
