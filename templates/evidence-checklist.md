# Substantial Merit Evidence Inventory

Use this checklist to inventory all third-party evidence of your impact and accomplishment. Be thorough and specific—vagueness is a red flag.

---

## Instructions

For each category below, list **specific, quantifiable evidence**. Don't estimate. Use actual numbers and documentation you can provide.

**Format example:**
❌ "I've published many papers"
✅ "12 peer-reviewed publications in journals with average IF 3.5, including [Journal 1], [Journal 2], [Journal 3]"
