# Sources

Every factual claim in this repository is grounded in one of the following categories. Claims that reflect analysis or interpretation are labeled as such in the relevant document.

---

## Primary Legal Authority

**Matter of Dhanasar, 26 I&N Dec. 884 (AAO 2016)**
The controlling decision for EB-2 NIW adjudication. Available through USCIS.gov and legal research databases. This decision establishes the three-prong test applied to all NIW petitions.

**Immigration and Nationality Act, Section 203(b)(2)(B)**
Statutory basis for the National Interest Waiver. Authorizes waiver of job offer and labor certification requirements. Available through Congress.gov.

**8 CFR Section 204.5(k)**
Regulatory framework governing EB-2 petitions. Available through the Code of Federal Regulations and USCIS.gov.

**Matter of Kazarian, 26 I&N Dec. 285 (AAO 2010)**
Prior standard referenced in Dhanasar. Provides historical context for the evolution of EB-2 NIW adjudication standards.

## Government Data Sources

The following agencies produce reports that candidates may cite as evidence of national importance under Prong II:

- National Institutes of Health (NIH) — research priorities, funding gaps
- Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) — public health data
- National Science Foundation (NSF) — research needs assessments
- Department of Labor, Bureau of Labor Statistics — workforce data
- Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) — health system analyses
- Government Accountability Office (GAO) — sector-specific investigations
- Congressional Research Service — policy analyses

These sources are used as examples of the type of documentation that supports national importance claims. Specific reports cited in any guide are identified in context.

---

## What This Repository Does Not Cite

This repository does not cite:

- Anonymous internet forums or discussion threads
- Immigration blogs that do not cite primary sources
- Unverified statistics or data without attribution
- Personal anecdotes presented as evidence
- USCIS internal communications not publicly released

Claims about RFE frequency or approval rates are not made in this repository. USCIS does not publish category-specific RFE statistics.
